celltype
(

) – Choice of unit cell. Can be either the unit cell
from self.structure (“unchanged”), the conventional cell
(“conventional”), or the primitive unit cell (“primitive”).
cartesian

bool
) – Whether the atomic positions are provided in
Cartesian or unit-cell coordinates. Default is False.

) – Whether the bandstructure path along the
